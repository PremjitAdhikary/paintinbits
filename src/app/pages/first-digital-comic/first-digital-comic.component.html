After a lot of avatars :) I had enough confidence to embark on a larger project. I always wanted to write a comic. I already had a fine story ready (At least I think it's a fine story). So I rolled up my sleeves and got to work.

I started working on this comics back in Nov 2009. Took lot more time and effort than I initially estimated, but I did complete in Jun 2010 and published on a website for my wedding that I had hosted on the now defunct Google Sites.

Sep 2011, I started reformating the stories (including this one) that I had put up on the site, intention was to arrange on individual pages that can be printed into a book. The final product presented here is after those revisions.

Fair warning, comics are best experienced on a laptop or desktop.


Just after the reformatting of this comics was done, I was reached out by Fried Eye, an online magazine, if they could publish my comics on their site as a Valentine's day special.

Ok

Got published on their 2012 Feb edition. Here's the Fried Eye article.
