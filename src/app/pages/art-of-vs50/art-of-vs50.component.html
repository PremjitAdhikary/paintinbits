For my 50th segment in Mesmerize, I had made a game.

This was one of my most complex projects till date. Try it out.



The basic idea was to symbolize the challenges I faced to build the 50 segments. Which is why VS 50 if you still don't get it!

My plan was to always make a platformer. I had originally thought of having a Samurai vs Ninja themed game. I had already created a Samurai and a Ninja character in Simulated Combat. I had thought of extending the same characters (maybe add a few faster moves) and Let that play out.



But there are just too many Samurai/Ninja themed games out there! So instead, I made one based on Indian Mythology.



Character study and design for the game.

I had half a mind to draw Lord Ram blue. Then decided against it. The bow itself is a techno/spiritual marvel. Concept being it powered by cosmic energy drawn in by the Lord.

Vanara is inspired from earlier Neanderthals. Nimble and wields Gada.

Jambuvan, obviously inspired by bears. Heavily armored and wields Bagh Nakh.

Vali, I wanted to show the alpha of the jungle. So the gorilla was my inspiration. To make him more menacing, I made him a dual weapon wielder.



Once the character designs were complete, creation of sprites and corresponding animation.
