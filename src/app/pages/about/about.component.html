About

Paint in Bits is an online repository of all my art that I have drawn and I will be drawing (no matter how crappy they are). The 'I have drawn' pieces of work will be added as and when I get my hands on them, few of them are in my hard drive somewhere, few of them I don't even know if I will be able to recover. Another reason for this site is to keep me motivated to keep drawing and add more art. The more I draw the better I get.

In this site you may find something that inspires you too to pick up the pencil and tap your creative side. There are also a few write ups where I have jotted down a few lines regarding the drawings.

I am Premjit Adhikary. I started drawing from a very young age. The place where I was born and raised had the norm of having children enrolled in some activity so that they get involved in something other than regular school work. Few of my friends went to music schools to learn instruments, few went for martial arts, I was put into drawing. I didn't go to any school, but I did have a teacher who would come to my place. He helped me with the basics and even trained me a bit in water colors. Then I had to change schools and move out and that was the end of my drawing lessons.

It was a long time before I got interested in art again. I did doodle in my classes (small transfomers here and there), but that was not much practice. It was only when I got my job that I started drawing again, no I don't get paid to draw, it started more as a break from my daily routine. Then I saw all these digital artists and slowly moved into drawing on computer than on paper.
