For my wedding I had hosted a website on Google Sites where I put up the invitation card, few pictures and also some stories on how we met and the likes. Later I collected all those stories and reformatted it in a way so that they can be printed as a book. The idea was to gift one to each of our families and keep one for ourselves.

The reformatting took almost a year to complete (Sep 2011 to Oct 2012). New content was added for the book. Existing stories were modified with new art work. The comics had to be upscaled to a bigger page size. After all the color adjustments, the book was printed.

Google Sites allowed small javascript to be added to it's page. I customised my site with small javascript code bits to have a comic viewer for the digital version of the book. The whole book was put up there on the comics viewer for anyone to read (if interested).

Then came 2021 and Google decided to stop Classic Sites from being available for public to view. The push was to move to the new Google Sites. The javascript comic viewer didn't work on the new site and I couldn't be bothered to debug it.

Fast forward to 2024 and I came across the pages archived on my Google Drive. I decided to upload them on Paint in Bits.
