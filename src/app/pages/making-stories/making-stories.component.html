It is 2025 and time for the next edition of Gogona. Last year I had submitted Inner Brahma which was well received. This time my submission was more of a fun reflection.

Unlike last year where I rushed to finish, I had a lot of time at my disposal to create this. Which is why this time I have some story going on.

Fair warning, comics are best experienced on a laptop or desktop.


I came up with a total of six stories for this collection. While the stories were inspired by actual events, the narrative put forward did not always reflect on what exactly happened.

  • A Cover Story: This is a legit retelling of what transpired between me and Chintanu.
  • A Thematic Story: Many discussions and debates on theme had occurred which is condensed in a single page here.
  • A Budget Story: I could not get any material on sponsors and budget before the due date for submission came up. So I had to make up a few potential sponsors!
  • A Selection Story: The keen reader might observe that I had let my imagination go wild here. I was not so sure about the 'program a random number generator' option, not all reader's would relate, but then I thought, Meh.
  • A Submission Story: Again a story based on conversations between me and Chintanu.
  • After Cover Story: This was more on the disagreement Bonnie and I had over the cover art. Again, a wild imagination. This story was not submitted for the magazine.

There were other events of interest in the making:

  • How our editor reached out to renown writers from Assam to pen a greeting for our magazine
  • Our editor ensuring deadlines are met

But just because something happened, doesn't mean that it can be moulded into a good story to tell.
