Why Avatars?

Remember Orkut? That's where I started with Avatars. Instead of putting in a jazzy profile pic, I started putting in some comic / cartoon version of myself there. Most of the Avatars were inspired by some event in my life. Somewhere I went, something I did, just kind of a fun way to put it out there. My friends found it quite entertaining in the way I put them out. So that too was a good motivation.

My first attempt at digital drawing. I thought what better than to draw myself? Big ego-boost I know. So why this? Well it looks like me. And why the crown? To answer that my friend, I will take up the next paragraph.

Before I explain the crown I need to explain why King Putai. Well the King explains the crown, so lets just say I explain the 'King Putai'. Ready for a history lesson? Here goes...

Long ago in an engineering college there were a handful of students who got hooked to an amazing game 'Age of Empires II'. They could played night and days at a stretch, only to go to classes in between. I was lucky to be part of that elite group. We got pretty good at the game and soon computer stopped providing us with much of a challenge. Then we started playing against each other, what a rush. We would be eager to reach our hostel from class and jump into the game.

So why are we discussing a early 2000 strategy game? Its because how we represented ourselves in the game. In the game, whenever we would play against the computer, it would have fancy names like King Henry III or General Khan or Emperor Ming. We started using similar names, I started using 'King Putai', Putai being my nickname. The name just stuck. I opened up an email account, my name was already taken, but kingputai was not. So it became my email id. That just continued everywhere, now I just like it. It's like my pseudo name. If you check my latest art, I even sign with the same.

Anyway, coming back to the Avatar, well as you can see as a first attempt, it was very crude, but I think it captured the essence of 'King Putai'. With my receding hairline, it was already a good caricature theme to begin with. Don't you agree?

My next few Avatars reflected my various experiences in US. To be frank, I had so many experiences from which I could have taken inspiration, but alas, time was never enough.

This was inspired by my trip to Chicago. Little me awed by the big tall buildings all around. Yup that summarized it pretty well.

Cedar Point, in Ohio, that was the Roller Coaster capital when you talk about amusement parks. I still remember the Top Thrill Dragster, a 420 feet high Hydraulic Launch Vertical Strata Coaster. I usually go for wooden Roller Coasters, but this was one steel Roller Coaster I would go every time I get a chance. Reaching about 120mph, it's still the best ride I have ever taken.

You can see the Dragster in the background of the Avatar for my Cedar Point trip.

White Water Rafting, and I don't know how to swim. Rafting is one of those adventures that you should do once in a lifetime. This was when we went to Yough River, Pennsylvania. No guide in our boat to supervise us, none of us with prior experience, we conveniently reaching late for the introductory session where they actually instruct on how to control the boat, more important, how to stay safe on the boat. Needless to say, my roommate fell off in the water, we pulled him in, crazy fun.

I was the person steering.

At first I have the Avatar that I created after my Smoky Mountains trip in Tennessee. Good green place to hike, beware of bears though. And next I have the one that I created after my Devil's Lake trip in Wisconsin. This was during the fall, hence the red/orange theme.

Next is on my first Miami trip, and it was rains the whole time. As you can see how disappointed I was on the empty beach. We regretted not checking out the weather earlier. The only plus, with all the day on our hands, well we got to sample so many places to eat near the beach.

Finally ... One of my best trips in US... Vegas. Everyone should go to Vegas at least once in their lifetime. So I had to create an Avatar for this, and what better way to showcase the place than a setup inside a Casino. Gave myself a Godfather look too. I had to put the crown somewhere, so I did that in my jacket pocket, instead of the usual triangle.

I started working on this during my LA trip. For some reason I could never complete it. And then I lost interest. I think I had a starry night as a background for this and neon signs in forground. I never put this out on Orkut.
