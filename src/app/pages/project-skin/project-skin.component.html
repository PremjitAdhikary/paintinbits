When I first bought my laptop, the same that I am using right now to create this page, I wanted to make it a bit different. I surfed and found a site which allows custom artwork to be applied as a sticker on the laptop. They call it Skin. All I had to do is upload an image (and pay $$), they would print that out on a sticker to the exact size of my laptop and ship it to me. That got me excited and I started off on a look that would be 'cool'. So again my ego ruled and I came to the conclusion that what better than to have 'me' on my laptop.

Here's the Skin that I came up with... Pretty cool right? Kidding!

One of the side products of this was a new Avatar which I started using as my login picture in my laptop. It also stayed as my Facebook profile picture for quite a while.

Another side product was an awesome wallpaper. Check it out.

I had a lot of fun working on the Skin Project. I had also created a small video out of it. I was helped by my then room-mate Harsh Mehta to create this video. He is good with the camera too and he took all the pictures during this project.
