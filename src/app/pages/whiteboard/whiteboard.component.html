While most of the people use the whiteboards in their office to jot down important things like To-dos, project pointers, everyday contacts, workflow etc, I usually would draw something on it and keep it for days before erasing it off and drawing something new. They were mostly inspired from existing artwork in and around that I tried with a marker. It was kind of a release to draw in office. Also gave my work area a personal touch. Too bad now I don't have a whiteboard in my current location.
