Drawing Sky is a good exercise. It's so random, you can't go wrong with it. Gone are the days when I would simply paint the the paper blue. Even during my PaintDotNet experience, I would do a simple gradient and get away with it. The viewers would not pay much attention to the Sky, it's like 'It's blue, and high, so must be the sky', kind of a reflex. So I decided it's high time I give some respect to this subject.

Originally I had planned for 4 pieces, sunrise, sunset, daylight and nighttime. But then sunrise and sunset, I thought 2 orange entries might make it redundant. So here we are, the 3 moods.
