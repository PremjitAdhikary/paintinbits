My college will celebrate Diamond Jubilee this year. Among many things, there is a plan to bring out a Souvenir. The committee in charge reached out to all students of Jorhat Engineering College to submit their articles/stories/poems/cartoons related to JEC and it's life.

I submitted a comics.

Regarding the art style, I kept it simple. There was a deadline by which this had to be submitted. So simple lines with mostly flat colors.

Fair warning, comics are best experienced on a laptop or desktop.

For this Diwali I had planned a section in Mesmerize to animate some fire crackers. This comic gave me a superb idea for a cross-over.
